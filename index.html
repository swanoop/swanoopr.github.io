About Me

As a Cybersecurity Engineer at Aegis Engineering Ltd, I specialize in operational technology (OT) cybersecurity. I hold an MSc in Forensic Science from Osmania University and an MSc in Cybersecurity and Forensics from the University of Westminster, which is certified by the National Cyber Security Centre (NCSC) and accredited by the British Computer Society (BCS). My academic background is complemented by practical experience and certifications, including ethical hacking (CEH v11). At Aegis Engineering, I focus on OT/ICS cybersecurity projects, conducting risk assessments and developing robust solutions tailored to these environments. I am a Professional Member of the British Computer Society (BCS), the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET), and the International Society of Automation (ISA), where I serve on the board of the ISA England Chapter. Additionally, my involvement with Young Rail Professionals highlights my commitment to fostering innovation within the rail industry. I am passionate about professional development and collaboration within the cybersecurity community.
